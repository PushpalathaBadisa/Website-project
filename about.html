about us

Gudlavalleru Engineering College (GEC) is a private university situated in Krishna, Andhra Pradesh permanently affiliated to JNTUK, Kakinada, and accredited with an ‘A’ Grade by NAAC. The college was established in the year of 1998 by Late Vallurupalli Venkata Rama Seshadri with only 180 students whereas the current take of the college is more than 1300 students in both programs.

Gudlavalleru Engineering College Campus has been well planned and constructed according to students’ needs and comfort. The College offers engineering courses as well as management courses and has separate & expensive-equipped labs and well-ventilated rooms for each course. The Library is as big as one cannot imagine with more than six lakh books. GEC campus also has a medical center available for 24 hours, a hygienic cafeteria, and Wifi facility.

Gudlavalleru Engineering College offers B.Tech, M.Tech, MBA. Admission to various courses offered by the GEC is done on the basis of the entrance examination. Various specialisation in B.Tech is offered include civil engineering, mechanical engineering, electrical, electronics, computer science engineering and information technology.
